About Me

A couple paragraphs about me:

Hi!! I'm Julia and I'm your resident Bay Area tech trash bro. Just kidding. But I did grow up primarily in Cupertino for more than half of my life so I've undoubtedly been influenced by the ever-changing technology trends born from the heart of the Silicon Valley and have used pretty much any consumer-facing tech product out there.

I don't know if I'd call myself a code junkie (following my earlier reference to being a tech trash; I also think I'm just a professional copy-paster), but I'm constantly learning more languages and tech stacks and challenging myself as well through the courses I take at UC Berkeley. In high school, I built and maintained websites for some of the clubs I was in and continued my passion for web design & dev in college, where I joined the course staff for a Web Design Decal at Berkeley. During breaks, such as winter or summer, I try to lose myself in a few personal projects as well, as listed below.

I also love travelling and vlogging (check out my Youtube channel!), exploring beautiful places and eating delish foods. I sometimes play video games in my free time and I also like urban dance. If I'm not doing any of the above, you'll probably see me drinking boba milk tea and/or working out, but obviously not at the same time.
